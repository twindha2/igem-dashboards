## Critical Issues & Feasibility Concerns

### 1. **HP Lead Stakeholder Interview Timeline Is Unrealistic**
- **Jun 15 – Jul 31:** 6-8 interviews must be completed in ~7 weeks
- **Reality:** Scheduling external stakeholders (faculty, industry, patients, bioethicists) requires 2-4 week lead times. Cancellations and rescheduling are common.
- **Impact:** May is likely to fall behind, pushing interview work into fall semester while managing courses.
- **Recommendation:** Extend Phase 2 (Engagement) by 2-3 weeks OR reduce interview target to 4-5 initial interviews with option to add follow-ups in fall.

### 2. **August Crunch—Peak Overlap for Both Roles**
- HP Lead: Final interviews (Aug 31), draft due (Aug 31), change log (Sep 1)
- Wiki Lead: Wet lab integration (Aug 2), draft v1 (Aug 23)
- **Reality:** Both leads are writing simultaneously with no buffer. One delay cascades (HP content needed for wiki; if HP draft is late, wiki draft slips).
- **Recommendation:** Separate timelines—HP draft due Aug 15, wiki draft v1 due Sep 1. Allow 2-week buffer between dependent tasks.

### 3. **Wiki Freeze During Fall Coursework**
- **Sep 25 wiki freeze** = 6 weeks into fall semester
- Students have NOT yet faced midterms (typically Oct 10-20) but ARE in the buildup phase
- **Sep 7 design polish + Sep 14 final review + Sep 25 freeze** = 3 deliverables in 3 weeks while balancing 3-4 courses
- **Recommendation:** Move wiki freeze to Oct 1 (earlier, before midterms) OR Sep 18 (allows polish work to complete in Aug).

### 4. **Missing iGEM Official Milestones in Dashboards**
The dashboards do NOT include:
- Team registration deadline (if applicable)
- Safety forms submission deadline
- Parts Registry submission deadline
- Judging form submission deadline (partially addressed)
- Official wiki freeze date from iGEM HQ (dashboards assume Sep 25, but verify with iGEM competition portal)

**Recommendation:** Add these as master deadlines to the Project Coordinator dashboard so nothing is missed.

### 5. **Dependency Chain Risks**

| Dependency | Risk | Mitigation |
|------------|------|-----------|
| HP feedback → Project changes | If interviews delayed, project changes don't inform wet lab work | Start preliminary interviews (May 15?) instead of Jun 15 |
| HP content → Wiki narrative | If HP draft late, wiki structure must wait | Separate timelines with 2-week buffer |
